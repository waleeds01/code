Lumpia

What Is Lumpia? Lumpia are fried spring rolls that are found in Filipino and Indonesian cuisines. The rolls traditionally feature a thin pastry skin (a.k.a. a lumpia wrapper) and are often stuffed with a savory mixture of ground pork and vegetables.

prep time

30-60min.

cook time

8 min.

serving

1 serve

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Desserts Dinner

instructions

step 1

Combine ground chicken, singkamas, carrots, green onions and celery in a medium bowl. Then, add oyster sauce, salt and pepper, sesame oil and 3 Tbsp of Ladyâ€™s Choice Mayonnaise; mix well.

step 2

Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the mixture into each lumpia wrapper. Roll tightly. Seal ends of the wrapper with water.

step 3

Deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot.

1/2 kilogram ground chicken
