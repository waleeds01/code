Tortang Talong

Tortang talong, also known as eggplant omelette, is an omelette or fritter from Filipino cuisine made by pan-frying grilled whole eggplants dipped in an egg mixture. It is a popular breakfast and lunch meal in the Philippines.

prep time

10min.

cook time

13min.

serving

6 serve

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Desserts Dinner

instructions

step 1

Pierce the eggplant with a fork and roast over open flame until skin is charred. Place in a bowl and cover. Set aside for 10 minutes. Peel the charred skin and flatten with a fork.

step 2

Season each eggplant with a pinch of MAGGI® Magic Sarap.

step 3

Beat an egg in a deep plate and season with a pinch of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Dip the eggplant in the beaten egg.

step 4

Fry eggplant with the egg until golden brown. Drain excess oil, transfer on a serving plate and serve.

ingredients

6pcs Eggplant

6pcs Egg

1 sachet MAGGI Magic Sarap 8g

3 cups Water
2tbsp Brown Sugar

0.5 cup Vegetable Oil

tools

Pan Fry

Plate

Gloves

Spatchula
