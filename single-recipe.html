Leche Flan

Leche Flan is a dessert made-up of eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top. It resembles crème caramel and caramel custard. This delicious dessert is known throughout the world.It has been a regular item in the menu of most restaurants because of its taste, ease in preparation and long shelf life.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30-35 min.

serving

1 serve

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Desserts Dinner

instructions

step 1

Mix together egg yolks, condensed milk, and evaporated milk. Pour into a caramel-coated 9-inch round baking dish

step 2

Bake the flan in a water bath at 350°F for 60-75 minutes until it's set but still slightly jiggly in the center.

step 3

Let the flan cool to room temperature, then chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight. Serve chilled.

ingredients

10 egg yolks

1 can (14 oz) condensed milk

1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk

1 cup granulated sugar

1/4 cup water

tools

Mixing bowl
Whisk or fork

Saucepan
9-inch round baking dish

Foil
Larger baking pan or roasting pan

Oven
Measuring cup and spoons
